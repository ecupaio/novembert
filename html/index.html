What is Novembert?

Novembert is the month in which Razorbacks Head Coach, Bret Bielema, aka BERT, makes a final push into bowl season. Since 2014, the month of November has marked the beginning of a hot streak for BERT and his Hogs. Last year's Novembert began with a last minute win over ranked Ole Miss on a 4th and 25 lateral play.
